Giving a Voice to Stray Animals

At StraySafe, we're on a mission to give voice to the voiceless, offering support and care to stray animals in need.

  • Connecting Compassionate Individuals

    We bridge caring individuals with local organizations, facilitating swift aid for neglected stray animals. Through our platform, making a difference in their lives becomes effortless.

  • Transparency and Impact

    We believe in transparency every step of the way, showing precisely how your contributions directly impact stray animals' lives. By showcasing the tangible difference your support makes, we foster trust and accountability within our dedicated community.

Join us in our endeavor to create a world where every stray animal receives the love, care, and support they rightfully deserve. Together, we can make a lasting and meaningful difference in the lives of these often overlooked members of our society.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to common queries about StraySafe and how our platform works.

  • How does StraySafe work?

    StraySafe allows users to report injured or needy stray animals by uploading a picture along with the animal's location. This information is then forwarded to the nearest NGOs, who provide assistance to the animal. Users receive feedback once help is provided.

  • How can I report a stray animal using StraySafe?

    You can report a stray animal by visiting our website and using the upload feature to send a picture of the animal along with its location.

  • How do you ensure privacy and security of user information?

    We prioritize the privacy and security of user data. Information shared on our platform is only used for the purpose of assisting stray animals and is not shared with third parties without consent.

  • How can I donate to NGOs through StraySafe?

    You can donate to the NGOs listed on our platform by visiting their profiles and following the donation instructions provided.You can also donate to the NGOs via us, and can check for the donation slabs available on our website for further details.

  • How can I volunteer with StraySafe or local NGOs?

    To volunteer, please reach out to the NGOs directly through their contact information provided on our platform.

  • Can I track the progress of my reported animal?

    Yes, you can track the progress of your reported animal by logging into your StraySafe account and checking for updates from the NGOs.
